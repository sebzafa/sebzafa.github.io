VCU PCB

LHR

×

Vehicle Cntrol Unit

This board is mainly used for distributing power to and controlling auxillary systems such as lights, fans, pumps, and other boards. It also has an IMU using isoSPI, CAN transceiver , a GPS module using UART, and a coolant sensor.
It's key design feature is being able to handle high current as as much as 40A may pass through it.
I designed schematics for the board, such as the power conversion sheet, the parent sheet, the connector sheet, power dist. sheet, and more.
I also layed out the board in its entirety being concious of signal degradation and accuracy in ares such as the usb-c connector and micro controller. Also, the current sensing for this board is susceptible to inaccuracies due to the high current passing so that was a consideration.

Steering Encoder VCP

Steering Rack Board

I wrote firmware, debugged, and tested a Steering encoder board that uses a magnet at the end of the steering assembly which is detected by a hall effect sensor on the board.
I wrote code and set up files using STMCubeMX, and STMCubeProgrammer enabling USB VCP and flashing, and wrote an algorithim to get accurate angles based on the magnet's orientation.

FRC CAD

FRC

×

FIRST Robotics CAD

I led the design of subsystems of a competition robot, particularly with the shooter, intake, and their respective mounts.
A lot of the design work revolved around quickly iterating as theres an incredibly short design window of about 2 weeks including prototyping and ideation
I was also the main driver of the robot during competition at which I worked with other teams and my drive team to maximize our match efficency through coordination and teamwork.
We went on to nearly upset the reigning state champions, NASA, and Robochargers, with whom we had one the State championship with the prior year losing 110-108 in the state semi finals.
At the world champinoship in Houstin, we were statistically the best passing robot in the world, and were selected by then reigning world champions, 4414 HighTide. Through FRC I learned alot about working with others project deadlines, CAD design, and leadership.
